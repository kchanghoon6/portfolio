ywc.kr — Youth Web Coalition

Founder & developer Flask · SQLAlchemy · SQLite · Vanilla JS live

YWC is a live information platform I founded and built for students at CheongShim International Academy — a single place that catalogues opportunities and college-prep resources across four modes, backed by heavy, hands-curated data.

594Competitions
868Pre-college programs
111Volunteering listings
40AP subjects

Four modes

  • Competitions — 594 curated competitions across 9 categories and 32 subcategories, with eligibility and pathways.
  • Pre-college — 868 summer/pre-college programs across 40 universities in 4 countries and 20 fields.
  • Volunteering — 111 volunteering opportunities across categories and regions.
  • CSIA — 40 AP subjects plus clubs and a roadmap tailored to the school.

Data quality is the hard part

The value of YWC is in trustworthy data, so the competition list went through many QA cycles — for example pruning from ~900 down to 594 by removing unverified, duplicate, ineligible (e.g. college-only or US-only) or undersized entries, and correcting hundreds of fields (organizers, URLs, age ranges, eligibility). I built the catalogue, the seed pipelines, and the four-mode browsing experience.

Tech

Flask + SQLAlchemy + SQLite on the backend, vanilla JavaScript and CSS on the front end. The platform is live and in active use.
